Refund Policy

This Refund Policy explains how refund requests are reviewed and handled for purchases made through moealturej solutions.

1. Digital Products

Because products may be delivered instantly or digitally, refunds may be limited or unavailable once access has been provided.

2. Eligibility

Refund requests may be considered in limited situations such as duplicate purchases, broken delivery caused by the platform, or other clearly valid issues.

3. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds may be denied for change of mind, lack of use, misunderstanding of the product description, or violation of terms.

4. Request Process

To request help, contact support through the Discord server and provide clear details about the order and issue.

5. Final Decision

All refund decisions are made at the discretion of moealturej solutions based on the circumstances of the request.
